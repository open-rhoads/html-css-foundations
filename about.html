About

Our First Hatch

Sunny Side Up is committed to serving you freshly made, quality breakfast & brunch dishes. We use cage free, organic eggs and heritage grade pork, and organic fruits & veggies in all of our meals. All of our bread and bakery items are fresh baked in house.

Our vision was born in 2016 when our founders began working with local farmers to source ingredients that might otherwise be sent to waste and put them to work in our kitchens instead. Come treat us with a visit and it won't take you long to see that the grass is always greener on the Sunny Side.
